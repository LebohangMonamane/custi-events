From Setup to Event Day in 6 Steps

Get your event live on WhatsApp in minutes. Here's exactly how Custi Events works.

Journey Overview

Run your event from the organiser portal while guests stay in WhatsApp

Custi Events gives organisers a structured setup flow, then turns WhatsApp into the channel where invitations, RSVPs, updates, and replies actually happen.

Set up once Create and configure the event in the portal
Go live on WhatsApp Use one dedicated event number for communication
Track every response Manage RSVPs and updates without guest app installs
Step By Step

How Organisers Launch an Event on Custi Events

Each step is simple on its own, but together they create a complete WhatsApp-first event workflow for organisers and guests.

1
Step 01 Organiser Portal

Create Your Event

Log in to the Custi Events organiser portal and use the guided setup wizard to create your event. Define the event name, type, date, venue, and guest capacity. Choose from event types including weddings, conferences, church events, corporate functions, and more.

Start with a structured event foundation
2
Step 02 Configuration

Configure Settings

Set up your RSVP rules, messaging preferences, seating configurations, and any custom fields. Configure how guests should respond and what information you need to collect from them.

Tailor the flow to match your event format
3
Step 03 WhatsApp Cloud API

Assign a WhatsApp Number

Assign a dedicated WhatsApp number to your event. This number becomes the communication channel for all guest interactions — invitations, RSVPs, announcements, and replies. Powered by the WhatsApp Cloud API.

Give every guest message one clear destination
4
Step 04 Guest Management

Upload Your Guest List

Import your guest list via CSV upload or add guests manually. The system validates your data, flags any issues, and gives you a clean summary before you proceed. Each guest is linked to their WhatsApp number.

Prepare clean guest data before launch
5
Step 05 Go Live

Activate Your Event

When you're ready, activate your event. Custi Events sends personalised invitation messages to all guests via WhatsApp instantly. You can monitor delivery, track RSVPs, and manage everything from the portal.

Send invitations and start collecting responses
6
Step 06 Guest Experience

Guests Interact via WhatsApp

Guests receive their invitation on WhatsApp and can RSVP, ask questions, and receive updates — all without downloading any app. They simply reply to the WhatsApp message. You see all responses in real time in the organiser portal.

Keep guest communication natural and low-friction

The result: organisers get structure and control, while guests get a fast, familiar communication experience inside WhatsApp.

Why WhatsApp?

WhatsApp has over 2 billion users globally. Your guests are already on it. By running your event communication through WhatsApp, you eliminate the need for guest apps, email open rates, and SMS costs — while getting near-instant delivery and read rates.
