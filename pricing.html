FAQ

Pricing Questions Answered

When does the paid tier kick in?
The paid tier applies when your event has more than 10 guests. Events with 10 or fewer guests are completely free.
What counts as a "message"?
Each WhatsApp message sent to a guest counts as one message. The paid tier includes 4 messages per guest in the base price of R10/guest. Additional messages can be purchased in bundles of 50.
Is there a monthly subscription fee?
No. Custi Events is priced per event, per guest. You only pay for what you use — no recurring subscription fees.
Can I upgrade from free to paid mid-event?
Yes. If your guest list grows beyond 10, the system will prompt you to upgrade. Your event data and settings are preserved.
What payment methods are accepted?
We support major payment methods. Contact us or request a demo for details on payment options available in your region.
